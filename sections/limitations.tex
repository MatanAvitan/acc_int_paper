\section{Limitations}

The main-text quantitative claims are deliberately limited to the core completed evaluation suite. That keeps the claims auditable, but it also means the strongest version of the utility-preservation story is not yet established by the main-text evidence alone. The deeper iterative sycophancy and safety comparisons are directionally consistent with the main narrative and are included in Appendix~\ref{sec:appendix_snapshots}, yet the paper's central claims still rely on the smaller completed suite.

A second limitation is measurement scope. All experiments use Qwen2.5-0.5B, and utility is measured through task-specific proxies such as answer matching, helpfulness ranking, profession classification, and dataset-source retention. These metrics are appropriate for surgicality, but they do not yet establish broad capability preservation across unrelated tasks.

Finally, the geometry diagnostics support low-dimensional structure, concept nonlinearity, and connectedness, but they do not by themselves prove that curved-manifold effects are the sole causal mechanism behind every leakage--utility tradeoff. The current paper therefore treats geometry as strong supporting evidence for tangent projection rather than as a complete causal proof.
