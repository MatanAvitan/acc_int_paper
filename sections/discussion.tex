\section{Discussion}

The headline pattern is consistent: the only method that matches or beats Obliviator on probe accuracy at every budget while damaging the control task less is the tangent-projected one (Tab.~\ref{tab:main_results_unified}, Fig.~\ref{fig:pareto_sota_obliv_floor}, Fig.~\ref{fig:per_model_bars_gender}). Two coupled mechanisms produce this pattern. The tangent constraint defines \emph{where} edits live: ambient updates carry components weakly aligned with the local representation support, and tangent projection removes them at first order. The local-$\sigma$ preconditioner defines \emph{how far} to move along each axis: each tangent direction is updated proportional to the manifold's local extent in that direction, which is exactly the singular value $\sigma_{i,k}$ already produced by the secant SVD. Together they restrict every step to a fraction of the local manifold-supported neighborhood. Surgicality on control concepts is the empirical signature: in the most-correlated CelebA regime, where the dominant tangent axes are shared between target and controls, the preconditioned step is the only one that lands inside the budget on every panel.

\paragraph{Limitations.}
The headline tables report a single deterministic seed=0 run; multi-seed variance across the 39 NLP panels will be reported in App.\ as the runs complete. Concept removal is judged by a fresh 2-layer MLP probe ($h{=}128$, 200 SGD), which captures recoverability under the protocol of \citet{elazar-goldberg-2018-adversarial} but not corpus-wide capability preservation; we do not run held-out general-domain perplexity here. The local manifold motivation is operational, not a theorem: TwoNN intrinsic-dimension estimates and secant-SVD bases are first-order approximations, and we do not connect tangent-estimation accuracy to erasure quality through a closed-form bound. Multi-layer erasure (re-estimating tangent spaces after each intervention) and richer local-manifold estimators are natural next steps.
