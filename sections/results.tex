\TanGCE{} is the only method whose overall mean approaches the floor at every budget ($+2.6 \to +0.4$pp from $\le 1$pp to $\le 10$pp); Obliviator plateaus at $+4.0$ then $+2.7$pp; \AmbGCE{}-vanilla leaves $+7$ to $+10$pp and fails to reach the budget on $6$ to $15$ of $39$ panels; LEACE alone leaves an $\approx 18$pp gap that closed-form rank-2 covariance matching halves. \subsection{Cross-modal generalization: CelebA dual control regimes}
\label{sec:celeba}

We re-run the same fixed-$\varepsilon=0.1$ pipeline on 80 CelebA-CLIP panels: 40 binary attributes $\times$ 2 control-set regimes (least- vs.\ most-correlated 5 attributes, by training-split Pearson $|r|$). The most-correlated regime is the surgicality stress test: erasing \emph{Male} while preserving \emph{Wearing\_Lipstick}, \emph{Heavy\_Makeup}, \emph{No\_Beard}, \emph{Attractive}, \emph{5\_o\_Clock\_Shadow} (all $|r| > 0.4$ with Male) shares dominant axes between target and controls.

\input{tables/celeba_per_panel_breakdown}

Tab.~\ref{tab:celeba_per_panel_breakdown} extends Tab.~\ref{tab:per_panel_breakdown} to CelebA: per-attribute signed $(S - S_{\text{floor}})$ at the closest-to-floor step subject to mean $\Delta Y \ge -5$pp, for all five methods on each of the 40 attributes under each regime. The least-correlated block is essentially a tie at floor for all methods except LEACE / LEACE+CovMatch K=2 ($+5$ to $+6$pp above floor). The most-correlated block separates them: only \TanGCE{} retains an admissible operating point on $38$ of $40$ attributes (vs.\ $30/40$ for Obliviator and $33/40$ for \AmbGCE{}-vanilla). On the heavily entangled panels Obliviator's first trajectory step already exceeds the $-5$pp budget by $10$ to $22$pp (\emph{Male}/most: every Obliv step in $[-21.1, -13.2]$pp), leaving no in-budget point at all. This is the empirical signature of the local-$\sigma$ preconditioner: when target and controls share the dominant tangent axes, weighting each direction by its local manifold extent is what permits an admissible step. The legacy 3-concept (Male, Smiling, Blond\_Hair) version of this comparison and the all-40 aggregate under global-$\lambda$ HPO are reported in App.~\ref{sec:appendix_celeba_clip_fair} and \ref{sec:appendix_celeba_extended}.
